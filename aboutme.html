Biography

Hi, My name is Daisy Nguyen. I was born in Berkeley and I've lived in Castro Valley for the majority of my life. I'm an incoming senior at Castro Valley High School. In school, I am an officer of two clubs in school: Teens Reaching Out and Girls Learn International. TRO is a community service club and GLI is a club that raises money and awareness for girls' health and education around the world. I am passionate about fighting for social justice. I am the first generation Vietnamese-American in my family.
